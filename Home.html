Our Mission

Ubuntu Rise Community Centre is a non-profit organisation built on the foundational African philosophy of Ubuntu — "I am because we are."

Our mission is to provide holistic, integrated support to the most vulnerable in our community.
We serve the elderly and frail, survivors of abuse, and those who are lost and without focus by creating a safe space that fosters dignity, connection, and mutual upliftment.

At Ubuntu Rise, we believe in the power of Ubuntu “I am because we are.”

In partnership with the Gift of the Givers Foundation, we are proud to serve as a safe haven and a place of growth for the most vulnerable in our community. Our centre is dedicated to uplifting the elderly, survivors of abuse, and those seeking new direction in life.

Through our core programmes Golden Years for seniors,Safe Harbour for survivors of abuse, and Pathfinders for life skills development we create spaces of dignity, care, and empowerment. Our social enterprise, The Rise Shop, helps sustain our mission while giving the community a chance to give back.

This website is your digital doorway into our hub. Here, you can learn about our programmes, connect with our team, access resources, volunteer, or support us in building a resilient, compassionate community where no one is left behind.
