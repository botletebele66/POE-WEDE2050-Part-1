Welcome to Ubuntu Rise Community Hub

Building a resilient community where no one is left behind, and everyone can rise and thrive together.

Our Mission

Ubuntu Rise Community Centre is a non-profit organisation built on the foundational African philosophy of Ubuntu — "I am because we are." Our mission is to provide holistic, integrated support to the most vulnerable in our community.

We serve the elderly and frail, survivors of abuse, and those who are lost and without focus by creating a safe space that fosters dignity, connection, and mutual upliftment.

Our Programs

1. Golden Years Day Program

A welcoming space where seniors can connect, stay active, and feel valued. From gentle exercise classes like yoga and walking clubs, to shared meals and social activities, the program helps reduce loneliness and promote healthy living. Our team also offers guidance with managing medications, ensuring peace of mind for families.

Practical Support

Volunteers lend a helping hand with small home repairs, gardening, and transport to medical appointments — enabling seniors to remain independent and comfortable in their own homes for longer.

Intergenerational Connection

We bring seniors and youth together through storytelling, skills exchange, and tech-help sessions. These bonds enrich both generations, fostering learning, laughter, and belonging.

2. Crisis Care & Counselling

We provide immediate, confidential support for those experiencing domestic violence, elder abuse, or other forms of harm. Our staff guide survivors through legal protection options and, where needed, connect them with safe houses.

Empowerment Workshops

Through financial literacy, job readiness, and self-confidence programs, survivors gain the tools they need to rebuild independence and rediscover their focus in life.

Trauma-Informed Care

Every staff member and volunteer is trained to provide compassionate, understanding support that prioritises survivors' mental health and emotional well-being.

3. Pathfinders Life Skills Programme

Designed for individuals facing homelessness, addiction, or long-term unemployment, this programme provides the guidance and tools needed to rebuild a meaningful life. It includes:

  • Life Coaching & Mentoring = One-on-one support to set personal goals and create a clear step-by-step plan.
  • Skills Development = Practical workshops such as basic computer skills, cooking on a budget, and CV writing.
  • Connection to Resources = Guidance in accessing addiction recovery programs, further education opportunities, or government grants.

Community Kitchen & Garden

A safe space to share nutritious meals while learning valuable skills in food preparation and gardening. Beyond meeting immediate needs, these activities are therapeutic, build confidence, and support long-term physical and emotional well-being.
