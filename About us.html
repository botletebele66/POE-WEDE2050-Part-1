Our Story

Ubuntu Rise Community Centre was established in partnership with the Gift of the Givers Foundation, built on the foundational African philosophy of Ubuntu â€” "I am because we are."

Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a resilient, organized community where no one is left behind, and where support is a mutual process that empowers every individual to rise and thrive.

Our mission is to provide holistic, integrated support to the most vulnerable in our community by creating a safe space that fosters dignity, connection, and mutual upliftment.

Our Team

We are a dedicated group of professionals and volunteers committed to making a difference in our community. Our team includes social workers, counselors, program coordinators, and many passionate volunteers who believe in the spirit of Ubuntu.

All our staff and volunteers are trained in trauma-informed care to support our clients with empathy and understanding.
